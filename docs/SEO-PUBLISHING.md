# Dessert(y) House — search and AI discovery publishing checklist

## What is already in the website
- Search title, description, Chennai service keywords and canonical URL.
- Bakery structured data (Schema.org) for search engines and AI crawlers.
- `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` generated at `/robots.txt` and `/sitemap.xml`.
- Product category pages for brownies, bento cakes, fondant cakes, bomboloni, cupcakes, donuts and birthday cakes.
- Admin/order tracking pages marked `noindex` to protect private customer/admin areas.

## Publish after deploying your final domain
1. In Vercel, attach a custom domain (recommended: `dessertyhouse.in`). Update the `metadataBase`, sitemap and robots URLs in the code from the Vercel domain to your final domain.
2. Open Google Search Console → Add property → verify the domain with its DNS TXT record.
3. In Search Console → Sitemaps, submit `https://YOURDOMAIN/sitemap.xml`.
4. Create and verify Google Business Profile as a **service-area Bakery**. Use “Chennai”, website URL, WhatsApp number and accurate opening hours. Hide your home address if customers cannot visit it.
5. Add the same website URL to Instagram, WhatsApp Business, Facebook Page, Justdial and Sulekha.
6. Request honest Google reviews from completed customers. Never buy reviews or create fake ones.
7. Add genuine new photos/posts and seasonal offers regularly. Each completed previous order should have an accurate description and customer permission before posting.

## Keywords to use naturally
Home bakery Chennai; homemade cakes Chennai; brownies Chennai; bento cakes Chennai; fondant cakes Chennai; custom birthday cakes Chennai; eggless cake Chennai; cupcakes Chennai; donuts Chennai; handmade cakes Chennai.

Use these naturally in captions, product descriptions, Google Business updates and real customer reviews. Do not repeat them unnaturally or claim “best” / “number one” without evidence.

## Honest expectation
No website or SEO provider can guarantee first place in Google or AI recommendations. Strong local results come from a verified Google Business Profile, consistent accurate listings, genuine reviews, useful product pages, real customer images, links/mentions and time.
